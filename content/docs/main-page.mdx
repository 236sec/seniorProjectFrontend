Welcome to the Crypto Portfolio Tracker main dashboard. This page provides a real-time snapshot of the cryptocurrency market, helping you stay updated on price movements and trending assets.

## Market Overview

The **Market Overview** table displays the top cryptocurrencies ranked by market capitalization. Use this to track the performance of major coins.

### Columns Guide

- **#**: The coin's rank by market cap.
- **Coin**: The name and ticker symbol (e.g., `BTC`) of the cryptocurrency.
- **Price**: The current market price.
- **24h %**: The percentage change in price over the last 24 hours. Green indicates an increase, while red indicates a decrease.
- **Market Cap**: The total market value of the coin's circulating supply.
- **Volume (24h)**: The total trading volume across all exchanges in the past 24 hours.

### Using Pagination

There are hundreds of cryptocurrencies available. To view more:

1. Scroll to the bottom of the **Market Overview** table.
2. Click **Next** or **Previous** to navigate between pages.
   > **Note**: The table displays 10 coins per page and supports up to 25 pages of results.

## Trending Coins

The **Trending Coins** section highlights cryptocurrencies that are currently popular or experiencing high search interest.

### Columns Guide

- **Coin**: The name and symbol of the trending coin.
- **Price**: The current market price.
- **24h %**: The percentage change in the last 24 hours.
